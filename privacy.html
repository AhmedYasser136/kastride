Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.
This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account info: name, email, password
  • Personal info: age (optional)
  • Workout data: programs, progress logs, statistics

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device info: device type, operating system, unique identifier
  • Usage info: usage time, features used
  • Technical data: error logs, performance data

2. Data Storage

  • Your workout data is stored on our servers to enable synchronization.
  • Data is securely encrypted and stored.
  • You can access your data from any device after logging in.
  • We protect your data using best security practices.

3. How We Use Information

3.1 To Provide and Improve Service

  • Manage your account and login.
  • Sync data across devices.
  • Improve app performance and add new features.
  • Fix technical errors.
  • Enhance app experience based on general usage trends.

3.2 Communication

  • Send workout reminders and notifications.
  • Inform you about updates and new features.
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests.

3.3 Development and Improvement

  • We may use non-personal usage data to improve the app.
  • Analyze the most used features to enhance experience.
  • Understand how users interact with the app.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

4.1 With Your Consent

When you manually share workout programs with friends.

4.2 Legal Requirements

  • Compliance with laws and regulations.
  • Responding to government requests.
  • Protecting our rights and user safety.

5. Information Security

We take measures to secure your information:

  • Password encryption.
  • Data encryption in transit and storage.
  • Strict access controls.
  • Regular security monitoring.
  • Regular data backups.

6. Data Retention

  • We retain basic account information (name, email).
  • Workout data is stored to enable synchronization.
  • Upon account deletion, all your data is permanently removed.
  • You may request a copy of your personal data anytime.

7. Your Rights

7.1 Access and Update

  • Access your account info in app settings.
  • Update or correct personal details.
  • Request a copy of your personal data.

7.2 Deletion

  • You may delete your account anytime from settings.
  • All your information is permanently deleted upon account removal.
  • To delete workout data, you can delete it manually or remove your entire account.

8. Children and Minors

  • Users aged 13 and above may use the app.
  • Under-13 users must be supervised by a parent or guardian.
  • Parents are responsible for monitoring child usage.
  • If we learn a child under 13 is using the app unsupervised, we will delete the account.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. You will be notified through:

  • In-app notification.
  • Email.
  • Login message.

10. External Services

The app may contain links to external services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

11. Data Responsibility Limits

  • We protect your data using best security practices.
  • If your data is lost due to technical issues, we'll restore it from backups.
  • We are not responsible for loss due to user negligence or lost passwords.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or inquiries about our privacy policy, contact us via:

  • Email: support@kastride.com
  • Website: www.kastride.com
  • In-app: "Support" section in settings

User Rights:
To exercise any of your account-related rights, please contact us by email. We will respond within 30 days.
